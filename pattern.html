Theme: Moonlit Night

We wanted our project to include a clean simple style, based on the clean rounded circular shape of the moon.
We decided we would like to use a deep blue background with light colored text, along with some coordinating colors for other elements to coordinate with the night sky.
We will also use a couple of images of the moon from Pexels or Pixabay. We also discussed using icons of the phases of the moon for the more prominent links.
This example is based off of the Screen Filler version of the Zen Garden project.
